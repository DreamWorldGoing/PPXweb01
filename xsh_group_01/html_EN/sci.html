Foreign media: the rapid development of 5G in China has brought favorable signals to the real estate market

Schroders, a global wealth manager, believes a surge in Chinese cities' data capacity will boost their property market prospects, according to foreign media reports.

　

Schroders said that of all the major countries in the world, China will be the country that makes the most money using fifth-generation mobile communications technology, which boosts the development of China's smart cities while also benefiting property companies investing in those cities.

　　

Foreign media pointed out that the launch of 5G in China will increase the value of urban land. As 5G projects in various cities are moving forward methodically, the construction of 5G trial networks and smart parks will maximize the use of land reserves between cities.

　　

China is at the forefront of the technological revolution, and it will produce an unprecedented amount of data." " As economic activity increases, there will also be increased demand for land, said Hugo Machin, co-head of global property securities at Schroders. In China, companies that own or operate data centers and network infrastructure will see such growing demand.

　　

In recent years, China's investment in 5G technology has led by a large margin, even surpassing the us $24 billion. "" China has built 350,000 base stations since 2015, compared with less than 30,000 in the United States," "CNBC said, citing data from Deloitte, one of the big four accounting firms.

　　

A recent report from IHS Markit projected that global 5G economic output could reach $12.3 trillion by 2035.

The rise of "Internet + healthcare"

In recent years, as the country "Internet +" policy, the positive response in all walks of life, the health care industry has suffered from emerging to the profound influence of the Internet in the Internet as a carrier and technical method of the medical information query, online consulting, remote consultation, remote disease treatment, and other forms of medical treatment service become the new trend of developing the industry. The ministry of health actively guides and supports the development model of "Internet + medical treatment", making formal medical services and senior doctors the main body of Internet medical treatment, so as to improve service efficiency and benefit more patients. By virtue of the advantageous resources and technologies of the Internet, the "Internet medical" platform can effectively integrate medical service resources, adhere to the patient-centered approach, collect medical information inquiry, disease risk assessment, online disease consultation, remote consultation, medical news and other information, and provide convenient and efficient diversified medical services. "Internet medical treatment" helps to solve the contradiction between the imbalance of medical resources and people's increasing medical demands, so as to make high-quality medical resources closer to patients and benefit more people. The Internet has changed people's way of life and work. The development mode of "Internet + medical treatment" is an inevitable choice for industry reform and innovation. It will gradually change the traditional medical treatment process and become a new direction and general trend of industry development.

The 2018 international air robot competition was successfully held in Beijing

Founded in 1991, the international air robot competition is an annual event sponsored by professor Robert from Georgia institute of technology and funded by the international association of unmanned aerial systems (AUVSI). It has been 27 years since the competition, and six generations of tasks have been completed, all of which are intelligent high-tech competition tasks gradually improved from automatic control to autonomous control. Robot technology development and application, to explore the air will be closely integrated in the field of scientific research and practical application, incentives and convenient in the asia-pacific region college students take an active part in international air robot contest, guidance, navigation and control branch of China aviation institute in 2012 to set up the competition of international Asia Pacific division, with the United States each year conference held at the same time. A year later, tsinghua university made a breakthrough and successfully completed the sixth generation of tasks.

The international air robot competition (IARC) aims to advance the advancement of uav technology by setting up challenging, practical and meaningful competition tasks. These tasks are almost impossible to achieve when proposed, and the world will benefit when they are eventually completed by airborne robots.
