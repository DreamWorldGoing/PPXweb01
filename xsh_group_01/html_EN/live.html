民生热门资讯

  • 2018-08-14

    In the first half of 2018, 233,600 urban jobs were created in guangxi

    The employment situation in our region remained stable, with 233,600 new urban jobs created. We increased social security, fully implemented the national insurance program, and covered 8.67826 million people in five social insurance programs, namely basic old-age care, basic medical care, unemployment, work-related injury, and maternity insurance.

  • 2018-05-11

    More than five million impoverished disabled people have been lifted out of poverty in the past five years

    Over the past five years, more than 5 million impoverished disabled people have been lifted out of poverty, and the two-subsidy system for disabled people has been fully covered throughout the country, benefiting 21 million disabled people, the China federation of the disabled said Tuesday. We will continue to strengthen the safety net for the basic livelihood of disabled people. By the end of last year, minimum living allowances had been provided for more than 9 million impoverished disabled people in both urban and rural areas.

  • 2018-05-24

    Central enterprises implemented more than 1,500 poverty alleviation projects in deeply impoverished areas in 2017

    In 2017, more than 1,500 poverty-relief projects were carried out. 590 million yuan was invested in poverty alleviation, and more than 4 billion yuan in social funds was introduced.

  • 2017-08-08

    We will vigorously develop education, which is more equitable and of higher quality

    Education is the cornerstone of national rejuvenation and social progress, and matters for the country's future. Since the 18th national congress of the communist party of China, general secretary xi jinping has made a series of important statements on the work of education, revealing the nature and development law of socialism with Chinese characteristics education, and pointing out the goal and direction of reform and development of education in China. In April this year, general secretary xi jinping visited guangxi and delivered important speeches on key areas and key links related to education development, such as promoting education resource equalization, building rural teachers' ranks, and ideological and political work in colleges and universities. He expressed his deep expectations for education career.
