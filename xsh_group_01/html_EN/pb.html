We will promote development through party building and strengthen party building through reform

After the 18th national congress of the CPC, the CPC central committee put forward an important decision to comprehensively deepen reform. The reform of the party construction system is not only an important content of comprehensively deepening reform, but also a fundamental guarantee to achieve the goal of comprehensively deepening reform. The realization of the "two centenary goals" and the Chinese dream of the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation ultimately depends on the party's strong leadership and party building. To a large extent, the level of party leadership and governance and the scientific level of party construction depend on the scientific maturity of party rules and regulations.

Never forget the original intention, and firmly establish the four "consciousness"

Learning xi jinping thought on socialism with Chinese characteristics for a new era requires us to firmly establish the "four senses". Xi jinping thought on socialism with Chinese characteristics for a new era is the latest achievement in adapting marxism to Chinese conditions. It is the crystallization of the practical experience and collective wisdom of the party and the people. Learn propaganda implement the party's 19 big spirit, xi jinping, the new era of Chinese characteristic socialism, the most important is to promote party organizations at all levels and party members and cadres firmly establish a political consciousness, overall situation consciousness, core, par, further firm ideal faith, maintain the authority of the party central committee with comrade xi as the core and the centralized and unified leadership.

Political consciousness

Political awareness requires every party member cadre to view, analyze and deal with problems politically.

The general situation consciousness

The awareness of the overall situation requires every party member and cadre to view problems consciously from the overall situation, put work into the overall situation to think and position, correctly understand the overall situation, consciously obey the overall situation and firmly maintain the overall situation

Core consciousness

The core consciousness requires every party member cadre to identify with the core in ideology, revolve around the core in politics, obey the core in organization and maintain the core in action.

Aligning with consciousness

It is required to keep abreast of the CPC central committee, of the party's theories, lines, principles and policies, and of the decision-making and deployment of the CPC central committee.

The road is confident

To be confident in our path, we need the whole party and the people of China to firmly believe that the path of socialism with Chinese characteristics is the only way to achieve socialist modernization and create a better life for the people.

The theory of confidence

Upholding theoretical self-confidence means that the whole party and the whole nation should firmly believe in the correctness and truth of the basic theory of marxism and the theoretical system of socialism with Chinese characteristics.

System of confidence

History since modern times has proved that the system of socialism with Chinese characteristics is the system that best meets the needs of China's socialist modernization and ensures the smooth development of all undertakings.

cultural confidence

To uphold cultural self-confidence is to arouse the party and the people's historical pride in the fine cultural traditions of China, and to firmly believe in the party's leadership in building the people into a modern socialist country and in the great cause of rejuvenation of the Chinese nation.

Hold high the great banner, firm the "four confidence"

Socialism with Chinese characteristics is the theme of all the party's theory and practice since the reform and opening up, the whole party must be holding the great banner of socialism with Chinese characteristics, firmly establish the socialist road with Chinese characteristics theory system of self-confidence, self-confidence, self-confidence, and to ensure that the party and national business always forward the way to the right direction." History and reality have shown time and again that only socialism can save China and only socialism with Chinese characteristics can develop China. Upholding and developing socialism with Chinese characteristics is the only way to realize the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation.
